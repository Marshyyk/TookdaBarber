Tookdabarber runs on word of mouth — parents bringing their kids in for a first haircut, regulars who never had to look twice, first-timers who walked in nervous and left with next week's appointment already booked.

Every cut starts with a conversation, not a stopwatch. Fades are lined by hand, beards are shaped to the face in front of them, and straight razor work is done the slow way, because that's the only way it's done right.
